Expense Tracking

Keep track of your spending and manage your budget effortlessly. Our digital banking platform offers advanced tools that categorize your expenses, analyze spending patterns, and help you stay on top of your financial goals.

  • Automatic categorization of expenses (e.g., groceries, entertainment, etc.)
  • Monthly and weekly spending reports
  • Set budget limits and track progress in real time
  • Get insights into your spending habits with personalized tips

With our expense tracking features, you can gain better control over your finances and make smarter financial decisions.
